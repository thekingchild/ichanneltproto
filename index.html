Who we are

We foster business success by enabling organizations to harness the power of digital technology.

IChannel Technologies is a full-cycle software and IT consulting company based in Ibadan, Nigeria. For over fifteen years we've helped founders, operators and public institutions start, run and grow their work online — from websites and mobile apps, to cloud infrastructure, to the internal tools that keep teams productive.

Our consulting experience lets us shape personalized answers to real business challenges, not templated ones.
